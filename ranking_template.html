This table shows the current ranking and assessment for 123 datasets that have been assessed for Contextual quality metrics up until 31st May 2016. Each dataset was given an aggregated value on the metrics that marked the final ranking. Click on the column header to rank the datasets based on single metrics. The metrics are:

  • P1 - Provision for Basic Provenance Information
  • P2 - Identifying the Origin of the Data
  • U1 - Human Readable Labelling and Comments
  • U3 - Presence of URI Regular Expression
  • U5 - Indication of Used Vocabularies
